I read this book the summer before I started college. The life of Feynman reinvigorated my love for science and discovery.

I chose this book. So I chose to be just like Feynman: a genius scientist and an even more genius teacher.

This was many years ago. Now, I am a Celebrated Scientist. I push the boundaries of AI. Make it faster. Make it smarter. Make it cheaper.

“I always do that, get into something and see how far I can go.”

And I did. And now, the AI Singularity is within grasp. Finally, we can replace humanity. Finally. Hmmm...

Maybe I should have chosen Brave New World and thought about the dangers of technology.
