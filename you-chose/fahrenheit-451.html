In a highschool english class, I was told to choose a book for a reading project. I chose this book because it was so short. Little did I know, this book's ending made me tear up.

I chose this book. So I chose to fight the terrors of mass media and censorship and all things selfish. I chose to live for others.

This was many years ago. I became Whatever the World Wanted Me to Become. I help who I can help. I am the least important person.

"Everyone must leave something behind when he dies..."

I am dying now. They will hold ceremonies and a funeral for me. I will be remembered by others. But what about me?

Maybe I should pursued my selfish interest in mathematics. What if I had chosen Where Math Comes From?
