I first listened to this book as an audiobook while riding the lightrail home. How ironic! The book ruthlessly criticizes modern technology including digital entertainment and high-speed transportation.

I chose this book. So, I chose to hate science and technology. Technology strips away individual identity.

This was many years ago. Now, I am a Recluse Living in the Wild. I built my own shelter. I hunt my own food. And I am happy.

"I don't want comfort. I want God, I want poetry, I want real danger, I want freedom, I want goodness. I want sin."

But I'm in pain. Constant pain. I'm tired. I'm tired of surviving. The wild is dangerous.

Maybe I should have chosen Of Mice and Men and sought a safer life.
