One summer, I listened to this book as an audiobook while doing chores: washing the dishes, cleaning the car, tidying the house...

I chose this book. So I chose to appreciate the small things in life. I chose to fear dreaming big.

This was many years ago. I became Just Another Person. And I'm content with my life.

Wake up. Work. Eat. Relax. Clean. Sleep. Repeat.

“Guys like us got nothing to look ahead to.”

Is this all that I am? At least I'm safe. But I'm bored. I'm lonely.

Maybe I should have chosen Fahrenheit 451 and learned how to lead a courageous, meaningful life.
