I read this tale at least two times. Once, begrudingly, as a child. Once again, grasping for meaning, as a freshman in college after studying hard for my first final exams.

I chose this book. So I chose to remain a child.

This was many years ago. Now, I am still the Same Child. I'm curious. I'm playful. I do what I please.

"It is the time you have wasted for your rose that makes your rose so important."

I have many roses. I wasted time in music, in origami, in friends, in love, in exploration, and in life.

I lived a life worth living, right? Just one thing.

Maybe I could have focused on one thing. Maybe I should have chosen Surely You're Joking, Mr. Feynman and became focused man of science.
